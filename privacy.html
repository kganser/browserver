Privacy Policy

Simpl.js gathers and stores information provided by users and client software of the Simpl.js service. Some of this information is made publicly available by way of a user's profile page and other services on the site. You may hide your contact information from this page via your account settings.

Simpl.js does not sell or share user information with third parties except in providing the services requested by the user, to investigate a violation of the Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law. Aggregate information on usage of the Simpl.js service may be collected for the purpose of improving the content or quality of the service.

Simpl.js may periodically update this policy. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or with a prominent notice on the site.

Cookies and Encryption

Simpl.js uses cookies to maintain session state while a user is logged in to the site. This and other data transmitted between the user and Simpl.js service is secured via HTTPS.

Credit Cards

Simpl.js does not store credit card information on its servers. This information is delivered to Stripe, which processes payments in accordance with PCI DSS.
